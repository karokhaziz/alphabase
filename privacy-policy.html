Privacy Policy

At Alphabase, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Business information including company name, registration details, and tax information
  • Contact information including email address, phone number, and physical address
  • Transaction data including sales, purchases, and payment information
  • User account credentials and access logs
  • System usage statistics and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our database management services
  • To process transactions and maintain accounting records
  • To send important notifications about your account and system updates
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To comply with legal obligations and business requirements

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Regular automated backups ensure your data is safely stored
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using secure protocols
  • We regularly update our security measures to maintain data protection

Data Retention

  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • Backup data is stored for a maximum period of 12 months
  • You can request data deletion subject to legal and business requirements
  • Transaction records are maintained as required by law

Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Export your data in a common format
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Request account deletion (subject to legal requirements)
